Our Mission

Our mission is to give Staten Island's residents, small businesses, and community organizations clear, trustworthy economic information so they can make better decisions for their future. By turning complex public datasets into accessible visuals, the dashboard helps people see how employment, transportation, and business activity are changing across the borough.

We believe that local data should be as easy to understand as a neighborhood conversation. This platform is designed to support everyday choices, opening a shop, hiring staff, applying for grants, or finding a first job by putting Staten Island's economic story in one place for everyone to use.
