About the Network

The Business Mentorship Network connects aspiring and current entrepreneurs with successful Staten Island business owners who have been through the challenges of starting, growing, and scaling a business in our community.

Whether you're developing your first business plan, facing operational challenges, or looking to expand, our mentors provide practical advice, industry insights, and valuable connections to help you succeed on Staten Island.

How It Works

Monthly Networking Events

  • Evening mixer events at local venues across Staten Island
  • Mix of structured presentations and open networking time
  • Meet mentors, entrepreneurs, and community partners
  • Casual environment perfect for building relationships

One-on-One Mentorship Matching

  • Complete a profile describing your business and goals
  • Get matched with a mentor based on industry and experience
  • Meet monthly (in-person or virtual) for personalized guidance
  • Flexible relationships - continue as long as helpful

Industry-Specific Roundtables

  • Quarterly deep-dive sessions for specific sectors
  • Topics like food service, retail, professional services, tech
  • Learn from successful businesses in your field
  • Problem-solve common industry challenges together

What Mentors Can Help With

  • Business Planning: Refining your concept, developing business plans, validating ideas
  • Operations: Day-to-day management, hiring, supplier relationships, workflow optimization
  • Financial Management: Budgeting, pricing strategies, cash flow management, fundraising
  • Marketing & Sales: Customer acquisition, branding, digital marketing, retention strategies
  • Legal & Compliance: Licenses, permits, contracts, insurance, regulations
  • Growth & Scaling: Expansion planning, new locations, product diversification
  • Leadership: Managing teams, work-life balance, decision-making, resilience

Who Should Join

This network is perfect for:

  • Aspiring entrepreneurs who want to learn before launching
  • Early-stage business owners (0-2 years in operation)
  • Growing businesses facing new challenges
  • Anyone who values learning from experienced business leaders
All industries and business stages welcome! Our mentor network spans retail, food service, professional services, healthcare, construction, tech, and more. Whatever your business, we can match you with relevant guidance.
