About the Clinic

In today's digital world, having a strong online presence is essential for local businesses. The Digital Presence Clinic provides hands-on help to Staten Island shop owners who want to reach more customers online but don't know where to start.

Our team of trained volunteers, including college students studying marketing and business, work one-on-one with local businesses to set up, optimize, and maintain their digital footprint.

Services We Provide

🔍 Google Business Profile

Claim and optimize your Google listing with photos, hours, and accurate information

🗺️ Maps & Directories

Get listed on Apple Maps, Yelp, and other local directories with consistent information

📱 Social Media Setup

Create and optimize Facebook, Instagram, and other social profiles for your business

⭐ Review Management

Learn how to respond to reviews and encourage satisfied customers to share feedback

🌐 Website Basics

Set up a simple, mobile-friendly website or improve your existing one

📊 Analytics Setup

Connect your online presence to analytics tools to understand customer behavior

What to Expect

Before the Clinic

  • Schedule a 30-minute assessment call to discuss your needs
  • Gather business information (photos, hours, services, contact info)
  • Bring laptop or tablet if you have account passwords to share

During the Clinic (2-3 hours)

  • Work one-on-one with 1-2 trained volunteers
  • Set up or optimize 2-3 key online platforms
  • Learn basic strategies for maintaining your presence
  • Get a custom action plan for next steps

After the Clinic

  • Receive written instructions for ongoing maintenance
  • Access to our resource library and video tutorials
  • Option to return for follow-up sessions
  • Join our business owner community group

Why Digital Presence Matters

According to our Staten Island economic data:

  • 78% of local shoppers search online before visiting a store
  • 92% read reviews before choosing a local business
  • 65% check business hours and location on Google Maps
  • Businesses with complete online profiles get 3x more calls and directions requests
It's not about being tech-savvy—it's about being findable. We'll handle the technical details so you can focus on running your business.

Who Should Attend

  • Retail shops and restaurants
  • Service providers (salons, repair shops, contractors)
  • Professional services (accountants, attorneys, consultants)
  • Any local business wanting to attract more customers

No technical experience required! If you can use email, you can manage your online presence with our help.
