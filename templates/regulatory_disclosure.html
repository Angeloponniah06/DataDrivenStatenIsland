Regulatory Compliance

Data Protection

We comply with the following data protection regulations:

  • CCPA: California Consumer Privacy Act
  • COPPA: Children's Online Privacy Protection Act
  • New York SHIELD Act: Stop Hacks and Improve Electronic Data Security Act
  • GDPR: General Data Protection Regulation (for EU visitors)

Employment Law Compliance

As an organization that provides employment information and resources, we adhere to:

  • Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) requirements
  • Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) standards
  • Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA) guidelines
  • New York State Labor Law

Website and Digital Services

Our digital services comply with:

  • Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act (accessibility standards)
  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA
  • CAN-SPAM Act for email communications
  • Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA)

Data Sources and Methodology

Data Collection

The data presented on our platform is collected from the following sources:

  • U.S. Census Bureau
  • Bureau of Labor Statistics
  • New York State Department of Labor
  • NYC Open Data Portal
  • MTA (Metropolitan Transportation Authority)
  • Local government agencies
  • Academic research institutions

Data Accuracy Disclaimer

While we strive to ensure the accuracy and timeliness of all data presented:

  • Data is provided "as is" without express or implied warranties
  • We rely on third-party sources and cannot guarantee complete accuracy
  • Data may be subject to revisions by source agencies
  • Historical data may be updated retroactively
  • We are not liable for decisions made based on the data provided

Data Update Frequency

Different datasets are updated on varying schedules:

  • Employment Data: Monthly
  • Business Statistics: Quarterly
  • Transit Data: Weekly
  • Demographic Data: Annually or as census updates are released

Funding Sources

Data Driven Staten Island is supported by:

Financial Transparency

As a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, we maintain financial transparency:

  • Annual financial statements are available upon request

No Commercial Activities

Data Driven Staten Island:

  • Does not engage in commercial data sales
  • Does not sell advertising space
  • Does not receive compensation for job listings or business profiles

Conflict of Interest Policy

We maintain strict policies to prevent conflicts of interest:

  • Board members and staff must disclose any potential conflicts
  • Decisions are made independently and in the public interest
  • We do not accept funding that compromises our independence
  • Data presentation is objective and unbiased
