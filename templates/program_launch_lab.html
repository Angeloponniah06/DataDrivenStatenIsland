Small Business Launch Lab

A structured 8-week program where early-stage entrepreneurs learn how to read neighborhood-level data, evaluate market demand, and use data dashboards to make informed business decisions

📊 Data-Driven 🎓 Educational 🤝 Networking 💼 Business Planning

About the Program

The Small Business Launch Lab is designed specifically for Staten Island entrepreneurs who want to start or grow their business using data-informed strategies. Whether you're opening your first storefront, launching an online service, or expanding an existing operation, this program gives you the tools and knowledge to succeed.

Over 8 weeks of evening sessions, you'll learn business fundamentals while working with real Staten Island economic data to make strategic decisions about location, pricing, and target markets.

What You'll Learn

Week 1-2: Business Model Fundamentals

  • Developing your business concept and value proposition
  • Understanding your target market and customer segments
  • Creating a lean business model canvas
  • Introduction to Staten Island economic indicators

Week 3-4: Financial Planning & Budgeting

  • Creating realistic financial projections
  • Understanding startup costs and operating expenses
  • Pricing strategies based on local market data
  • Funding sources and grant opportunities

Week 5-6: Location Strategy & Market Analysis

  • Using the Data Driven dashboard to analyze neighborhoods
  • Evaluating foot traffic, demographics, and competition
  • Understanding transit accessibility and parking
  • Commercial real estate basics

Week 7-8: Licensing, Operations & Launch

  • NYC business license and permit requirements
  • Legal structures (LLC, Corporation, Sole Proprietorship)
  • Hiring and workforce considerations
  • Marketing and digital presence
  • Final pitch and business plan presentation

Program Benefits

  • Expert Mentorship: Work with successful Staten Island business owners and consultants
  • Peer Network: Connect with other entrepreneurs in your cohort
  • Data Access: Use our economic dashboard for ongoing market research
  • Resource Library: Templates, guides, and tools for business planning
  • Pitch Opportunity: Present your business plan to local investors and partners
  • Follow-up Support: 6 months of check-ins and office hours after graduation

Who Should Apply

This program is ideal for:

  • Aspiring entrepreneurs with a business idea
  • Existing business owners looking to expand or pivot
  • Recent graduates interested in starting a business
  • Anyone passionate about contributing to Staten Island's economy
No prior business experience required! We welcome participants from all backgrounds and industries. Our program is designed to meet you where you are and help you grow.

Success Stories

Maria's Coffee Corner - Started as a mobile coffee cart concept in the Launch Lab, now a thriving café in Stapleton with 5 employees.

TechFix SI - Used dashboard data to identify underserved neighborhoods and opened three electronics repair locations across the island.

Island Artisan Bakery - Leveraged program connections to secure local supplier partnerships and retail distribution.
